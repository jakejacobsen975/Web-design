Emergency Contact Infomation

If there is an emergency please contact the right people for what is happening. If you find a dead animal on or close to the road call 911 or Utah Highway Patrol at 801-887-3800. If while you are on the trail and you see someone shooting in a conservation area or maybe someone is hunting in an area please contact DWR conservation officers as soon as possible at 1-800-662-3337. Also, there is a DWR Law Enforcement app on ios and android if you prefer to contact them that way. For any other reasons that you don't feel that 911 is the right number to call, instead, you can call Police Dispatch at 435 627-4300.
